Añejo Catering Co. SMS Program

Order & delivery updates · Palm Beach County, Florida

Añejo Catering Co. sends text messages so you always know where your food is — order confirmations and delivery updates (for example, "your order is on the way" and "your order was delivered"), plus account and service notices. These are transactional, relationship messages — not marketing blasts.

How to opt in

Opting in is your choice and is not a condition of any purchase. You opt in by entering your mobile number and checking the consent box — on this page below, or on our order, subscription, tasting-request, or wholesale forms. The box is unchecked by default; checking it is your express written consent to receive the texts described here.

Get Añejo order & delivery texts

Enter your number and check the box to opt in. We'll only text you the updates described on this page.

Message frequency varies — you may receive a text for each order and delivery. Message & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel anytime. Consent is not a condition of purchase. See our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Program details

  • Program name: Añejo Catering Co. order & delivery alerts.
  • Message types: order confirmations, "out for delivery" and "delivered" updates, and account/service notices.
  • Message frequency: varies based on your activity — you may receive a message for each order you place and each delivery. It is not a fixed number per month.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile plan. Añejo does not charge for the messages themselves.
  • Help: reply HELP to any message, or contact us at 561-567-1047 / dayan@anejocateringco.com.
  • Opt out: reply STOP at any time to cancel. You'll get one confirmation and no further messages.
  • Privacy: we never sell, rent, or share your mobile number, SMS opt-in, or text-messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for their marketing. See our Privacy Policy.

Sample messages

  • "Añejo Catering Co.: Your order is out for delivery and on the way today. Reply STOP to opt out."
  • "Añejo Catering Co.: Your order was just delivered — enjoy! Reply STOP to opt out."

Carriers are not liable for any delayed or undelivered messages. Full program terms are in the SMS & text messaging section of our Terms of Service.
