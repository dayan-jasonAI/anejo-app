The Story Behind the Brand

It started with family.

Añejo Catering Co. was created out of a real moment in our family — my mom received a difficult diagnosis, and at the same time I was changing how I trained, ate, and lived. We needed food that supported her health AND tasted like home. Nothing on the market did both.

"We weren't going to choose between flavor and longevity. So we built something that doesn't make you."

What grew from that necessity is Añejo today: a premium Cuban-American food brand designed to support a healthier lifestyle, inspired by Mediterranean-style eating patterns associated with heart health and longevity, made with nutrient-conscious ingredients — and grounded in the flavor, texture, and soul of authentic Cuban cooking.

From handcrafted bowls to signature bites, every plate is built with intention. Healthy doesn't have to taste like a cleanse. It can taste like home.

— DAYAN DIAZ, FOUNDER

Good to Know

Frequently asked.

Where do you deliver, and when?
We deliver across Palm Beach County, Monday through Saturday, in two windows — lunch (11:00 AM–2:00 PM) and dinner (5:00 PM–8:00 PM). Choose your window at checkout.
How fresh are the bowls, and how do I store them?
Every bowl is made fresh — never frozen — with about a 3-day refrigerated shelf life. They're microwave- and cold-friendly, with the signature sauce on the side so you control the moment.
Can you accommodate allergies and dietary needs?
Bowls may contain wheat, egg, milk, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, or seeds depending on the recipe, and are prepared in a shared kitchen. Nuts are removable on request and dairy-free substitutions are often available. Please tell us about severe allergies before ordering — see each bowl's label for full disclosure.
How do the weekly meal-plan subscriptions work?
Pick a weekly tier — 5, 10, or 12 bowls — and we deliver on your schedule. Subscriptions renew automatically each week and you can cancel anytime; see our Refund & Cancellation policy.
Is the AI macro calculator medical or nutritional advice?
No. The macro calculator and any generated plans are for general fitness and wellness information only — they are estimates, not medical or dietary advice. Consult your healthcare provider before starting any new nutrition program.
How does pricing and tax work?
Bowl and drink prices are shown on the menu; applicable Florida and Palm Beach County sales tax is added at checkout. Payments are processed securely by Square.
I'm a trainer or gym — can I partner with Añejo?
Yes. Our trainer portal lets you generate macro-targeted bowl plans for your members, who subscribe to Añejo directly. Ask us about our trainer & gym partner program. Explore the trainer portal →
